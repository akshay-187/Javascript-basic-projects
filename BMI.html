Body Mass Index (BMI) evaluates weight relative to height. BMI does not measure body fat directly and is not a perfect diagnostic tool.

For adults, a BMI below 18.5 is underweight, 18.5–24.9 is healthy weight, 25.0–29.9 is overweight, and 30.0+ is obese.

Adult BMI Chart Ranges
BMI Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal / Healthy Weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 – 34.9 Moderately Obese
35.0 – 39.9 Severely Obese
40.0 and above Morbidly / Extremely Obese
